FAQ

Will huge exports freeze the browser?

A soft limit (~100MP) helps avoid memory issues. Lower the resolution if needed.

Offline support?

Yes. Everything runs locally in the browser. For full offline, add as PWA or bookmark.

为什么选择黑色壁纸？

黑色壁纸简约克制、降低眩光；在 OLED/AMOLED 屏幕上，纯黑像素关闭，有助于省电并获得更深邃的对比。
